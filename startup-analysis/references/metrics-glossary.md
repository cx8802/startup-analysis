# Metrics Glossary

## Market Sizing

- TAM: Total Addressable Market. The broad total demand if the product captured the whole category.
- SAM: Serviceable Available Market. The portion reachable by the chosen product, geography, and customer segment.
- SOM: Serviceable Obtainable Market. The realistic share the startup may capture in a defined period.

## Revenue and Customer Metrics

- ARPU: Average Revenue Per User in a period.
- ARPA: Average Revenue Per Account in a period.
- AOV: Average Order Value.
- Paid Conversion Rate: Share of active users or leads that become paying customers.
- Churn: Share of users or revenue lost in a period.
- Retention: Share of users or revenue retained in a period.
- Activation Rate: Share of users who reach the first meaningful value moment.

## Unit Economics

- CAC: Customer Acquisition Cost.
- LTV: Lifetime Value. State formula and assumptions whenever used.
- LTV/CAC: Ratio between lifetime value and customer acquisition cost.
- Gross Margin: Revenue minus direct variable costs, divided by revenue.
- Contribution Profit: Revenue minus variable costs and acquisition costs.
- Payback Period: Time needed for gross profit or contribution profit to recover CAC.

## Modeling Guidance

- Use ranges when inputs are uncertain.
- Keep assumptions visible.
- Avoid using TAM alone as proof of opportunity.
- Treat early-stage forecasts as scenarios, not commitments.
