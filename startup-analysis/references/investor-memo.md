# Investor Memo Template

## Company

- Name:
- One-liner:
- Stage:
- Geography:
- Founder/team notes:

## Thesis

Summarize why this could become a meaningful venture.

## Why Now

Explain timing: technology shift, regulation, behavior change, cost curve, platform shift, or market opening.

## Market

Describe market size, growth, segmentation, and profit pool. Mark uncertain numbers clearly.

## Product and Wedge

Explain the initial product, beachhead customer, and why this wedge can expand.

## Competition

Map direct competitors, indirect substitutes, and status quo alternatives.

## Business Model

Summarize pricing, sales motion, margin profile, and key unit economics.

## Growth

Describe acquisition channels, activation, retention, expansion, and any loop effects.

## Risks

List the top risks and the diligence questions needed to reduce uncertainty.

## Milestones

Define the next milestones that would increase conviction.

## Recommendation

State whether to proceed, watch, pass, or request more validation, with reasons.
